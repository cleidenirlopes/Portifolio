    The Guardians of the Voiceless 🐾

    Connecting Shelter Dogs with Communities


    The Guardians of the Voiceless is a data-driven project designed to connect shelter

    dogs with community centers like retirement homes, schools, and veteran associations in Los Angeles.

    By integrating Petfinder's real-time animal data with the Google Maps API,

    the project identifies potential matches between shelter dogs and organizations

    that can benefit from therapeutic animal visits.

    The goal is to promote mental health, reduce loneliness, and support animal

    welfare while increasing adoption rates and community involvement.

    This initiative showcases how data integration can create meaningful

    social impact, benefiting both people and animals.
